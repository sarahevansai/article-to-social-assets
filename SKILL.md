# Article → Social Assets

Convert any article into 5 professional, branded social media promotion images in 2 minutes.

## Quick Start

1. **Share the article URL** — any article, any format
2. **Paste your pull quote** — the key statement you want highlighted
3. **Provide branding source** — URL where we pull your logo and brand colors (e.g., your company homepage, LinkedIn profile, brand guidelines page)
4. **Done** — 5 ready-to-post images saved and ready to upload

## What You Get

- **LinkedIn hero** (1200×630) — professional layout with quote, branding, article context
- **X/Twitter card** (1024×512) — compact version optimized for Twitter
- **Instagram Story 1** — quote feature with branding
- **Instagram Story 2** — key insights or breakdown
- **Instagram Story 3** — call-to-action / full article link

All images include:
- Your actual branding/logo
- Professional typography and layout
- Dark theme with accent colors
- Safe zones for Instagram Stories
- Clear attribution/CTA

## Usage

When you have an article you want to promote:

```
I want to promote this article: [URL]

Pull quote: "Your direct quote here"

Branding URL: https://yoursite.com or https://linkedin.com/company/your-company
```

The skill will:
1. Extract article metadata (title, description, author, publication)
2. Pull branding/logo from your provided URL
3. Design 5 professional assets
4. Save high-quality PNG files ready to post

## Notes

- **Works with any article** — interviews, press releases, thought leadership, announcements, etc.
- **Branding sources** — company homepage, LinkedIn company page, brand guidelines page, or any URL with your logo
- **Quote selection** — paste exactly what you want featured (the skill handles text wrapping/sizing)
- **Quick turnaround** — 2-3 minutes from URL to ready-to-post files
- **Download & share** — all images are optimized for their respective platforms
